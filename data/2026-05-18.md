### [1] [Holographic Airy Beamforming: Curved Trajectory Optimization for Blockage-Resilient Terahertz Communications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16057)
*Xinyuan Hu,Boya Di,Lingyang Song*

Main category: eess.SP

Relevance: 2/5

Why: 该论文聚焦太赫兹通信的波束赋形和遮挡规避问题，利用RHS生成Airy波束，涉及6G物理层和天线设计，但与CSI获取、信道估计、CSI反馈/压缩或AI方法的核心研究方向关联较弱。

TL;DR: 该论文针对太赫兹通信的近场遮挡问题，提出利用可重构全息表面（RHS）进行波前工程，生成沿抛物线轨迹传播的Airy波束以绕过障碍物。通过RHS可调有效孔径增强设计自由度，提出基于幅度控制的空域Airy波束赋形方案和几何轨迹优化算法，仿真表明相比传统移相阵列可提升受阻用户接收功率10 dB以上。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Matched topics: 6G无线通信, 太赫兹通信, 可重构全息表面RHS, 波束赋形

Motivation: 解决太赫兹通信中近场聚焦波束易受障碍物遮挡的问题，利用波前工程生成Airy波束绕过遮挡。

Method: 利用RHS（辐射单元间距小于半波长）的可调有效孔径实现Airy波束的抛物线轨迹设计；提出基于幅度控制的空域Airy波束赋形方案；开发基于几何的轨迹优化算法最大化受阻用户接收功率。

Result: 相比传统相控阵模拟波束赋形，RHS方案可提升受阻用户接收功率超过10 dB。

Conclusion: RHS凭借其可调有效孔径，在生成近场Airy波束以规避太赫兹遮挡方面具有显著优势。

Abstract: Terahertz communication offers vast bandwidth for high-speed transmission in the 6G networks but faces severe blockage challenges in the near-field region due to large antenna arrays. To overcome the limitation that near-field focused beams are susceptible to obstacles, wavefront engineering is leveraged to generate an Airy beam that propagates along a parabolic trajectory to circumvent blockages. In this paper, we consider the reconfigurable holographic surface (RHS) as a potential solution for such precise wavefront engineering owing to its compact radiation element spacing being much smaller than half-wavelength. We reveal that the adjustable effective aperture of the RHS allows the parabolic offset to be located within the antenna aperture, which enhances the freedom in designing Airy beam trajectories. An analog beamforming method, named the holographic Airy beamforming scheme based on amplitude control, is then proposed to generate the curved beam that propagates along the desired trajectory. To maximize the received power of a blocked user, we develop a geometry-based trajectory optimization algorithm. Simulation results validate that, compared to traditional phase-controlled arrays with analog beamforming, the RHS can leverage its adjustable effective aperture to improve the received power of the blocked user by over 10 dB.

### [2] [Fundamental Performance Limits of Non-Coherent ISAC: A Data-Aided Sensing Perspective](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16196)

Main category: cs.IT

Relevance: 2/5

Why: 本文关注ISAC系统的速率-感知失真分析，涉及未知CSI下的性能表征，但与AI辅助信道估计/重建/反馈/压缩等核心研究方向无直接关联。

TL;DR: 本文研究了双基地MIMO ISAC系统中，在接收端未知CSI的条件下，导频辅助感知（PS）与数据辅助感知（DAS）两种方案的通信速率-感知失真折中，利用随机矩阵理论推导了DAS方案的渐近闭式表达式，揭示了DAS相比PS在低SNR下3dB有效SNR增益和在高SNR下更快的性能缩放速率。

Motivation: 在ISAC系统中，接收端通常未知信道状态信息，如何在有限导频开销下实现高效的感知与通信联合优化是一个关键问题。本文旨在刻画未知CSI条件下PS和DAS方案的速率-失真性能边界，并量化DAS相对于PS的增益。

Abstract: In this paper, we investigate a bistatic multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) system over block-fading channels, focusing on the scenario where the sensing and communication receivers (Rxs) are co-located. Under the assumption of unknown channel state information (CSI) at the Rx, two schemes are considered: pilot sensing (PS) and data-aided sensing (DAS). The communication rate-sensing distortion functions for both schemes are characterized. For the DAS scheme, a closed-form asymptotic expression for the sensing distortion is derived by using random matrix theory (RMT). The asymptotic performance analysis explicitly quantifies the significant gains of the DAS scheme, revealing a strict $3$ dB effective SNR improvement in the low-SNR regime and a strictly faster performance scaling rate in the high-SNR limit compared to the PS scheme.

### [3] [Statistical two-round search for one excellent element](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15612)
*Nagananda K G,Jong Sung Kim*

Main category: cs.IT

Relevance: 1/5

Why: 该论文研究组合搜索问题的统计版本（Katona问题），与无线通信中CSI获取、信道估计、AI辅助物理层等核心研究方向没有直接关联。

TL;DR: 本文研究Katona两轮搜索问题的统计版本：在n个元素中（每个元素以λ/n概率独立为"优秀"），通过两轮无噪声子集测试，以至少1-α的概率找到一个优秀元素，目标是最小化期望测试次数。结果表明，成功受限于没有优秀元素的可能性（α ≥ e^{-λ}），在可行时最优期望测试次数随n对数增长。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Matched topics: 

Motivation: 研究统计版本的Katona两轮搜索问题，即在稀疏泊松机制下以最少测试次数找到至少一个优秀元素，这是对经典组合搜索问题的概率扩展。

Method: 结合存在性测试（第一轮判断是否存在优秀元素）与第二轮分离设计（寻找具体优秀元素），并利用信息计数论证推导下界。

Result: 在稀疏泊松机制下，成功概率的可行性条件为α ≥ e^{-λ}；在可行条件下，最优期望测试次数随人口规模n对数增长。

Conclusion: 给出两轮搜索问题的统计版本的信息论下界和可达上界，揭示了在稀疏优秀元素场景下测试次数的对数标度律。

Abstract: We formulate and study a statistical version of Katona's two-round search problem of finding at least one excellent element in a set. A population of $n$ elements is considered, where each element is independently excellent with probability $λ/n$, $λ> 0$. A subset test is noiseless: it returns positive exactly when the queried subset contains at least one excellent element. The goal is to minimize the expected number of tests subject to finding one excellent element with probability at least $1-α$, where $0<α<1$, under the restriction that testing is performed in two rounds. Unlike classical group testing, the objective is not to recover the full set of excellent elements, but only to identify one of them. We first show that success is fundamentally limited by the possibility that no excellent element exists. In the sparse Poisson regime, this imposes the necessary feasibility condition $α\ge e^{-λ}$. When the target success probability is feasible, we prove that the optimal expected number of tests grows logarithmically with the population size. The upper bound is obtained by combining an initial existence test with a second-round separating design; the lower bound follows from an information-counting argument. Numerical illustrations show the feasibility boundary and the resulting logarithmic scaling.

### [4] [Breaking the Finite-Sample Barrier in Entropy Coupling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16229)
*Shahab Asoodeh,Jun Chen*

Main category: cs.IT

Relevance: 1/5

Why: 论文纯信息论理论，研究耦合与熵的基本性质，未涉及任何无线通信、信道估计、CSI获取/反馈/压缩或AI方法。

TL;DR: 本文提出最小列表熵耦合（minimum list entropy coupling）概念，研究在给定边际分布约束下，通过允许观测变量间任意依赖关系来打破有限样本障碍。发现独立观测使不确定性指数衰减，而依赖观测可在有限样本后完全消除不确定性（零熵），并给出了零熵的充要条件和贪心算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Matched topics: 

Motivation: 经典信息论中，给定边际分布下的联合分布构造通常假设观测独立，导致残余不确定性指数衰减但无法归零。本文旨在探究允许观测间任意依赖时，能否在有限样本下完全消除不确定性。

Method: 形式化定义最小列表熵耦合问题，推导零熵的充要条件与结构准则，设计具有单调逼近保证的贪心算法，并将框架应用于分布匹配表示学习和随机性提取。

Result: 依赖观测可在有限样本后实现零熵（精确消除不确定性），需要 O(log(1/P_min)) 个观测；独立观测则只能指数衰减。给出了零熵的充要条件和结构判据。

Conclusion: 该框架统一了分布匹配学习和随机性提取中的有限样本极限问题，零熵对应精确恢复和精确提取。

Abstract: Dependence among marginally constrained observations can break a finite-sample barrier. To formalize this phenomenon, we introduce the \emph{minimum list entropy coupling} $H(P\|Q_1,\dots,Q_m)$, the minimum conditional entropy $H(X|Y_1,\dots,Y_m)$ over all joint distributions with prescribed discrete marginals $X\sim P$ and $Y_i\sim Q_i$. Unlike classical formulations based on independent observations, our model allows $Y_1,\dots,Y_m$ to be arbitrarily dependent while keeping each marginal fixed. This enlarged coupling space reveals a sharp dichotomy: independent observations reduce residual uncertainty exponentially, whereas dependent observations can eliminate it exactly after finitely many samples. We characterize this zero-entropy regime through necessary and sufficient conditions and give concrete structural criteria under which it occurs. In particular, under mild support assumptions, zero entropy is achieved with $O(\log(1/P_{\min}))$ observations, where $P_{\min}$ is the minimum nonzero mass of $P$. We also develop a greedy algorithm with monotone approximation guarantees for computing $H(P\|Q_1,\dots,Q_m)$. Finally, we show that the same framework formalizes finite-sample limits in distribution-matching representation learning and randomness extraction, where zero entropy corresponds to exact recovery and exact extraction.

</details>
